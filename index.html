Requirements

Ensure you meet the following requirements to use our service

1. Valid ID

All passengers must present a valid government-issued ID at the time of boarding.

2. Booking Confirmation

Ensure you have a booking confirmation via email or SMS before your ride.

3. Payment Method

Have a valid payment method ready, including credit/debit cards or UPI.

4. Punctuality

Please arrive at the pickup point at least 5 minutes before the scheduled time.
